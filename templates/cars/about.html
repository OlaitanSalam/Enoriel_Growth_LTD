About Enoriel Growth Limited

Enoriel Growth Limited is an automotive importation and trading company focused on high-turnover, high-demand Toyota Corolla vehicles, delivering predictable margins through disciplined execution and transparent operations.

The company operates a fully integrated, end-to-end vehicle importation and sales model, managing the entire value chain—from vehicle sourcing in Canada to final sale in Nigeria.

Our operating philosophy emphasizes capital efficiency, margin protection, risk management, and execution discipline, enabling repeatable performance and sustainable growth.

Operating Strengths

Proven Market Focus

Concentration on Toyota Corolla models with consistent demand and strong resale performance in Nigeria.

Capital Efficiency

Fast inventory turnover enables continuous capital recycling and stable cash flows.

Risk Management

Comprehensive insurance, verified counterparties, conservative pricing assumptions, and contingency buffers.

Transparency & Governance

Detailed cost analysis, clear profit projections, structured repayment planning, and full transaction documentation.

What We Do

Vehicle Sourcing & Selection

We identify and verify quality Toyota Corolla and other vehicles from trusted Canadian dealers, focusing on models with proven resale demand, competitive pricing, and strong margin potential.

Purchase Execution & Export Documentation

We manage vehicle purchases, confirm titles and ownership, and coordinate all export and shipping documentation in collaboration with verified logistics partners.

International Shipping & Insurance

We oversee ocean freight from Canada to Nigeria, including shipment tracking and comprehensive insurance coverage to protect capital during transit.

Customs Clearing & Regulatory Compliance

We handle customs processing, duty payments, and regulatory requirements through vetted clearing agents to ensure compliance and smooth clearance.

Local Distribution, Marketing & Sales

Vehicles are transported to Lagos, marketed through established dealer networks, sold efficiently, and converted to cash to complete each operating cycle.
